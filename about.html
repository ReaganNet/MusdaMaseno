About Us

“A place to grow spiritually and serve faithfully”

Our History

Maseno University Seventh-day Adventist (MUSDA) Church was established in the early 1990s as a vibrant, student-led congregation within the heart of Maseno University. Founded to meet the spiritual needs of students, faculty, and the surrounding community, MUSDA has grown into a dynamic center for worship, fellowship, and outreach. Over the decades, the church has nurtured countless young leaders who have gone on to serve in various capacities within the Seventh-day Adventist denomination and beyond. From humble beginnings in small campus gatherings to a thriving community hosting impactful evangelistic campaigns, MUSDA remains committed to spreading the gospel and preparing for Christ’s soon return.

Our Mission

The mission of MUSDA Church is to proclaim the everlasting gospel of Jesus Christ through vibrant worship, intentional evangelism, meaningful fellowship, dedicated discipleship, and compassionate service. We aim to equip our members with the spiritual tools to live out their faith, foster a sense of community, and prepare the world for the imminent return of our Savior. By engaging students and the broader community in Bible-based programs, we seek to inspire a lifelong commitment to Christ and His teachings.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a Christ-centered, Spirit-led church that exemplifies biblical values and transforms lives. We aspire to be a beacon of hope within Maseno University and the surrounding region, fostering spiritual growth, encouraging servant leadership, and promoting unity among believers. Through dynamic worship services, active ministry involvement, and community outreach, MUSDA Church seeks to reflect God’s love and prepare hearts for eternity.

Our Values

At MUSDA Church, our core values are rooted in Scripture and guide our actions and interactions:

  • Faith: We uphold unwavering trust in God’s Word and His promises.
  • Community: We foster a welcoming and inclusive environment for all.
  • Service: We are committed to serving others with compassion and humility.
  • Discipleship: We prioritize spiritual growth through Bible study and prayer.
  • Evangelism: We actively share the gospel to reach souls for Christ.

Our Leadership

MUSDA Church is led by a dedicated team of student leaders and faculty advisors who are passionate about advancing God’s kingdom. Our leadership structure includes a church pastor, elders, and department heads who oversee various ministries such as music, youth, health, and community services. Guided by the principles of servant leadership, our team works collaboratively to ensure that every member and visitor experiences the love of Christ. We encourage active participation from all members, fostering a culture of shared responsibility and spiritual accountability.
