Music Ministry

The MUSDA Church Music Ministry is dedicated to glorifying God through the sacred art of music, uplifting souls and drawing hearts closer to Him. Our Church Choir, the cornerstone of this ministry, specializes in classical sacred music, including traditional hymns and anthems that inspire reverence and spiritual renewal. Guided by the belief that music is a divine gift, we aim to create an atmosphere of worship that reflects the beauty and majesty of God’s presence.

Church Choir Schedule

  • Sunday Practice: 3:00 PM at GSQ
  • Monday Bible Study: 6:30 PM at Millennium Complex

Biblical Inspiration

Psalm 95:1 – “O come, let us sing unto the Lord: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation.” (KJV)

Colossians 3:16 – “Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom; teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord.” (KJV)

Our Musical Mission

Our choir performs classical sacred music, from timeless hymns to choral anthems, carefully selected to elevate the worship experience. As Ellen G. White writes in The Voice in Speech and Song, “Music is of heavenly origin. There is great power in music to impress the mind with the sublime truth of God” (VSS, p. 421).

She further emphasizes, “Singing is as much an act of worship as is prayer. Indeed, many a song is prayer” (VSS, p. 423). Our music ministry embraces this principle, using classical arrangements to create a worshipful atmosphere that honors God and uplifts souls.

Featured Hymns

SDAH 83: O Worship the King

O worship the King, all glorious above,
And gratefully sing His wonderful love;
Our Shield and Defender, the Ancient of Days,
Pavilioned in splendor, and girded with praise.

SDAH 474: Take the Name of Jesus with You

Take the name of Jesus with you,
Child of sorrow and of woe;
It will joy and comfort give you,
Take it then where’er you go.
