Give to Support Our Mission

The Joy of Giving

At Maseno University SDA Church, we believe in the biblical principle of giving as an act of worship and stewardship. Your generous contributions support our mission to spread the gospel, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community.

“Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.” – 2 Corinthians 9:7

Your gifts enable us to fund ministries, community outreach, and church operations, ensuring that we continue to be a beacon of hope and faith.

“Bring the whole tithe into the storehouse, that there may be food in my house. Test me in this,” says the Lord Almighty, “and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven and pour out so much blessing that there will not be room enough to store it.” – Malachi 3:10
